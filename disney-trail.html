DISNEY TRAIL
Get ready to start your epic road trip across the country from Seattle, WA to Disney World!
Click the car icon on the map to start.

Begin your adventure on an amazing road trip.

  1. To begin your journey, click the car on the map in Seattle, WA.
  2. You will then have to answer trivia questions to get to your final destination, Disney World!
  3. If you answer the question correct, the car will move on the map to the next location.
  4. If you answer the question wrong, the car will remain in the same place until you answer the question correct.
  5. If you reach the final destination, you will be welcomed with a spectacular show.
